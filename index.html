Our Workation to Crete

In what we can now officially call a tradition, the team at A Bigger Circle goes on workation together every year. This year, we chose the beautiful island of Crete to combine productive work sessions with team-building activities and relaxation.

During our stay, we held daily workshops focused on our upcoming projects, brainstormed new service offerings, and enjoyed the local cuisine and beaches. These workations have proven invaluable for strengthening our team bonds and sparking innovation.
